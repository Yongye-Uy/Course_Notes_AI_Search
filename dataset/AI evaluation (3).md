What is Artificial Intelligence?
Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science that aims to create
machines capable of intelligent behavior.
In computer science, AI is defined as the study of "intelligent agents": any device
that perceives its environment and takes actions that maximize its chance of
successfully achieving its goals.
